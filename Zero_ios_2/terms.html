3. Description of Service

Zero Inbox is an AI-powered email action recommendation app that:

  • Connects to your Gmail account via Google OAuth (read-only access)
  • Classifies emails as operational (Mail) or promotional (Ads)
  • Suggests relevant actions based on email content
  • Provides quick access to common email actions (reply, schedule, shop, etc.)
  • Learns from user feedback to improve classification accuracy
