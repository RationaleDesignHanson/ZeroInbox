⚠️ Important iOS Constraint:

iOS app icons cannot have actual transparency. The "glassy transparent" effect is simulated using light gradients, subtle shadows, highlights, and texture overlays. The background must be opaque.

✨ Goldschläger Inspiration:

The golden sparkles are inspired by the suspended gold flakes in Goldschläger liquor. They should float throughout the translucent glass, creating an elegant and luxurious feel.
