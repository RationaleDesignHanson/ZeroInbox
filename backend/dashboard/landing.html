Frequently Asked Questions

Can you read my emails? +
NO. Our architecture makes this technically impossible. Emails flow through our system for processing but are never stored. We cannot see your email content even if we wanted to.
How does the AI work? +
We use Google Gemini to classify emails and suggest actions. Email content is processed in memory and immediately discarded. Google doesn't store your emails either. It's similar to Gmail's own AI features.
What email providers do you support? +
Currently Gmail and Microsoft Outlook/Office 365. More providers coming soon based on user demand.
Is this iOS only? +
Yes for now. Android version is planned after we perfect the iOS experience. Join the waitlist to be notified when Android launches.
How do I delete my data? +
Simply logout from the app or revoke OAuth access in your Google/Microsoft account settings. All your data is automatically deleted. We only store OAuth tokens‚Äîno email content.
How much does it cost? +
Free during beta! After launch: Free plan (100 emails/day), Pro ($10/month unlimited), Team ($25/user/month with admin dashboard).
